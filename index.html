Instruments I like to play

I am expert in playing the instruments mentioned below.

GUITAR

The guitar is a stringed instrument with which sound is produced by "plucking" a series of strings running along the instrument's body.

Drums

A drum is a musical instrument which produces sound by the vibration of a stretched membrane.

Mouth Organ

A mouth organ is any free reed aerophone with one or more air chambers fitted with a free reed.

Violin

The violin is a string instrument which has four strings and is played with a bow.
